Exhibition 1: Sound and Vision: The Art of Album Covers

This exhibition is an expansive visual journey through the history of album artwork, exploring how graphic design, photography, and illustration have shaped musical identity and cultural movements from vinyl to the digital age.

Exhibition 2: Synthetic Realities: Art, VR, and the Extended Self

This exhibition is an immersive journey into virtual and augmented reality environments crafted by leading new media artists, examining how extended reality technologies are transforming perception, identity, and the boundaries of the art experience.
